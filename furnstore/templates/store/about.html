Talpyn

Talpyn Furniture Factory is the production of sofas and wholesale sales from Almaty to all regions of Kazakhstan and other countries. Guarantee

Now our upholstered furniture can be purchased in many cities of Kazakhstan, Russia, Belarus, Lithuania, Latvia and Estonia.
